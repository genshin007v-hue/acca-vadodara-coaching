ACCA Professional Pathway

Detailed explanation of BT... (A business is like a big group of people working together. Everyone has a role just like players in a team. This subject explains how that team works. You learn what a business actually is. A business exists to earn profit and grow. But it also has responsibilities. It must treat employees fairly. It must follow rules and laws. It must care about society. You learn different types of businesses. Some are small. Some are large companies. Some are owned by one person. Some are owned by many people. You learn about managers. Managers plan and control work. Leaders inspire people. Leadership is about guiding others. Communication is very important. People must share ideas clearly. You learn about business structure. Like hierarchy and departments. Each department has a job. HR manages people. Finance manages money. Marketing promotes products. Technology is also included. Businesses use computers daily. Technology improves speed and accuracy. You learn about risks. Every business faces problems. Like competition or financial loss. You learn how to manage risks. Ethics is also very important. It means doing the right thing. Even when no one is watching. This subject builds your foundation. It helps you understand the real world.)

Detailed explanation of MA... (This subject is about managing money inside a business. Imagine you have pocket money. You plan how to spend it. That is budgeting. Businesses do the same. They plan income and expenses. You learn about costs. Cost means money spent. There are many types of costs. Fixed costs stay the same. Variable costs change. You learn how to calculate cost per unit. This helps in pricing products. You learn about profit. Profit means earning more than cost. Businesses always aim for profit. You learn break-even point. It tells when profit starts. Before that, business is just covering cost. You also learn decision making. Managers use numbers to decide. Like whether to produce or not. Or whether to outsource. You learn about performance. Is the business doing well? Numbers help answer that. Budgets are compared with actual results. Differences are called variances. Managers analyze variances. This helps improve performance. This subject improves thinking skills. You learn to analyze and decide. It is very useful in real life.)

Detailed explanation of FA... (This subject is like writing a record book. Every transaction is recorded. If money comes in, it is recorded. If money goes out, it is recorded. This is bookkeeping. You learn double entry system. Every transaction has two sides. Debit and credit. You learn about accounts. Assets are things owned. Liabilities are things owed. Income is money earned. Expenses are money spent. You prepare financial statements. Profit and loss account shows profit. Balance sheet shows position. These are used by outsiders. Like investors and banks. They want to know performance. You learn accounting rules. These rules ensure consistency. You also learn recording methods. Like journals and ledgers. This subject builds strong basics. It is very important for ACCA.)

Detailed explanation of LW... (This subject is about rules of business. Every business must follow laws. Laws keep things fair. You learn about legal system. You learn contract law. A contract is an agreement. It must be valid. You learn company law. How companies are formed. And how they operate. You learn rights and duties. Of directors and shareholders. You learn about fraud and negligence. And what happens if rules are broken. This subject protects businesses. And ensures fairness.)

Detailed explanation of PM... (This subject improves decision making. You learn advanced costing. You analyze performance. You compare plans and results. You learn key performance indicators. These measure success. You also learn forecasting. Predicting future performance. Managers use this to improve business.)

Detailed explanation of TX... (This subject explains taxes. Governments collect tax. You learn income tax. Corporate tax and VAT. You calculate tax amounts. You learn tax rules. And how to comply. This is important for businesses.)

Detailed explanation of FR... (This subject builds on FA. You prepare advanced accounts. You follow international standards. You analyze financial performance. You understand complex transactions.)

Detailed explanation of AA... (This subject is about checking. Auditors verify financial statements. They ensure accuracy. They check risks and controls. They build trust.)

Detailed explanation of FM... (This subject is about managing money. You learn investment decisions. Risk and return. Financing options. You help businesses grow financially.)

Detailed explanation of SBL... (This subject focuses on leadership. You solve real case studies. You make strategic decisions. You think like a CEO.)

Detailed explanation of SBR... (This subject is advanced reporting. You apply knowledge in real scenarios. You use judgment. You explain financial information.)

Detailed explanation of AFM... (This subject covers advanced finance. You manage risk. You evaluate investments. You make strategic financial decisions.)

Detailed explanation of APM... (This subject improves performance. You analyze strategy. You evaluate business success. You advise management.)

Detailed explanation of ATX... (This subject is advanced tax. You plan taxes. You advise clients. You ensure compliance.)

Detailed explanation of AAA... (This subject is advanced audit. You handle complex audits. You apply ethics. You give professional opinion.)
